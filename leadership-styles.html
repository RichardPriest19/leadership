The 8 Styles of Leadership

No single leadership style works in every situation. The best leaders understand all eight — when to use them, when to switch, and why the wrong style in the wrong context can undermine even the best strategy.

All 8 Styles
01

Vision-Driven

These two styles lead through a compelling picture of the future. They are most effective in organisations that need direction, energy, and a reason to change — and in leaders who can genuinely embody the vision they project.

02

People-Centred

These three styles place the human being at the centre of leadership. They build trust, unlock potential, and create the psychological safety that drives high performance. Most effective when talent retention, engagement, and long-term capability matter.

03

Adaptive

These three styles are defined by their responsiveness to context. Rather than applying a fixed approach, adaptive leaders shift their style based on the team's readiness, the task at hand, and the performance expectations in play.
